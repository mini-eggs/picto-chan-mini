About

This is a utility to browse 4chan/4channel threads. It's used by replacing the "boards.4channel.org" part of a thread's URL with "chan.evanjon.es" after replacing you'll be brought to a "slideshow" type display of the media within the thread.

Keybinds

  • H - back
  • L - next
  • J - volume down (webm)
  • K - volume up (webm)
  • SHIFT - download
  • SPACE - toggle playback (webm)
